CHICKEN PARM

DESCRIPTION

Chicken Parmesan is a heartwarming dish that fills the belly and ellates the tastebuds. It's an amazing balance between a crispy chicken and some warm, creamy, mozarella cheese. This is one of my favorite dishes of all time and makes my day so much better after just one bite.

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken thigh
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Bread crumbs
  • Pasta of your choice
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Parmesan Cheese

STEPS

  1. Make sure your chicken is even all over, this will help in the breading and frying process. Even your chicken with a mallet
  2. Breading your chicken. Breading can be achieved with flour, egg wash, then panko crumbs
  3. Do a light fry in a medium pan with oil, chicken should be finished golden brown
  4. In a seperate pan add your fried chicken cutlets, a generous amount of tomato sauce and top it off with mozarella cheese, parmesan cheese, and basil
  5. Once the mozarella is nice and melted, the chicken is ready to serve! Serve with your choise of pasta or garlic bread
