PALAK PANEER

DESCRIPTION

Palak Paneer with fresh spinach is an amazing and healthy dish that is served primarily in India. Palak in english is spinach and paneer is cubed cottage cheese. Palak can be prepared in multiples ways with potato, beef, lentils and just plain too. Its an amazing vegetarian dish that is served with fresh naan or can also be served as a side dish instead of a main course.

INGREDIENTS

  • Spinach
  • Paneer
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green Chili
  • Onions
  • Heavy Cream

STEPS

  1. Add salt and spinach into a pot of boiling water. Blanch the leaves until they are wilted
  2. Blend the spinach with giner, garlic, and green chili. Blend into a puree
  3. Add the spinach, onions, and oil into a pan and sautee
  4. After the sautee add in your desired spices and Pnaeer and cook
