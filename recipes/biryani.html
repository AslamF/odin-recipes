Biryani

Description

Biryani is a traditional dish in south asia which is bursting with spcices and flavor. It is a dish used to celebrate joyous times and good news. It is not a lengthy recipe but requires subtle care and expertise in preparing it. -----

Biryani can be prepared in different ways. There are chicken, beef, vegetarian and even fish options. A very important ingredient for BIryani is saffron, which is also very expensive. The dish can however, be prepeared without saffron as well.

Ingredients

  • Yogurt
  • Garlic Paste
  • Biryani Masala
  • Salt
  • Turmeric
  • Red Chili Powder
  • Lemon Juice
  • Chicken/Beef/Fish
  • Basmati Rice
  • Onions

STEPS

  1. Marinate your chicken with the collected spices and herbs in a big bowl
  2. Let your chicken marinate for however long you wish but at least more than 2 hours
  3. Rinse and drain your basmati rice, it is optional to add saffron to the rice after it is dried
  4. While the rice is sitting and the chicken is marinating prepare a pot with oil and similar spices, bring to a low simmer
  5. Add onions into the pot and continue simmering
  6. Add the chicken to the pot while it is simmering, cover the pot and let it sit
  7. Add yogurt and some more spices based on your preference, mix everything together well
  8. Add 3 cups of water and then your rice
  9. Let the rice and chicken cook for a short period of time with the lid on
  10. After some time, lift the lid and mix everything, at this point its ready to serve!
